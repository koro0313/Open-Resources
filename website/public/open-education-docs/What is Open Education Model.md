# What is Open Education Model

This new education system is designed to be totally community driven, and fixes many problems from the current system that we all are complaining about constantly.

> *Please check the table of contents in the sidebar to read the complete documentation for each pillar and aspect of this model.*

## This Model Has 4 Main Pillars

### 1. Open Resources
An attempt to make education as accessible as possible.

* An open source community driven platform with completely free resources for anyone to educate themselves on any subject.

### 2. Open Evaluations
My take on replacing typical tests by a more meaningful and reliable way to evaluate someone's expertise.

* Students can prepare themselves at their own pace and opt for an evaluation when they are ready.

### 3. A New Schooling System
A schooling system where students have freedom to explore different subjects and study what interests them at their own pace. Schools will be expected to inspire them, support them and provide them with whatever they need to grow.

* On the other hand, schools will primarily focus on every student's character development, confidence, sports, etc... teach them finance, and prepare them for real world.

### 4. A New Reservation System
A system where agents will have to visit students in person & issue a reservation, irrespective of caste and entirely based on their condition & needs.

* If any misused reservation is found we can trace back to the agent and check every other reservations they issued. This way it will be risky & hard to abuse reservation and everyone can be given a fair ground.

## The Problems This Model Solves

* Automatically filters out outdated syllabus.
* Top quality and completely free study resources for everyone.
* Ensure everyone gets a fair chance to education.
* Anyone of any age can educate themselves.
* Fixes the abuse of reservation.
* Ensures no one unworthy or corrupted survives in the system.
* Gives students the freedom to explore their interest & grow.
* Prepares students for the real world.
